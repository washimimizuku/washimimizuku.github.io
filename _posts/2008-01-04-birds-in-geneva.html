--- layout: post title: Birds in Geneva --- One of the cool things of being in Geneva, is that there are excellent bird watching spots inside the city. Not only because 1/4 of the city is made of public parks, but because there is a lake and two rivers inside the city. I have seen already about 50 different species around Geneva, and there are still a lot more to be seen.

My favourite 3 spots until now, are La Perl du Lac, Parc La Grange, and the spot where the two rivers meet. But I still have a lot to explore, so I bet I'll some other nice surprises.
