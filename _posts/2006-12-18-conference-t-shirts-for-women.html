--- layout: post title: Conference t-shirts for women --- Today I was remembered about something I wanted to blog about for a long time.

You know, women have different bodies than man have. Fortunately, I would say. And I would say they feel really discriminated when they go to a conference, and get a t-shirt made for a man. That's really sad. I hope my wife gets a nice female t-shirt when we go to the Lift Conference (this is not a critique to the Lift Conference, it's just an example since we are going there in February. This applies to all conferences that don't do t-shirts for women).

Yes, it's just a t-shirt. But discrimination is in the sum of little things.
