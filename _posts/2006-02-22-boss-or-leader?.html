--- layout: post title: Boss or Leader? --- Being a boss is much easier than being a leader. A real leader inspires people. A real leader motivates people. A real leader finds the gifts of each one and helps them find the best way to use them. A real leader shares all happy and sad moments. A real leader creates relationships with his followers. A real leader is an example to his followers, and instead of telling how to do, shows how to do.

The boss, on the other hand, only has to tell people what to do.

Churches should have leaders, not bosses.
